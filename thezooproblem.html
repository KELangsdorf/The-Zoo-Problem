  1. Mella

Monkeys


Our three monkeys are all brothers!
Please Note: Cookie is the oldest and only eats berries. Earl is very friendly and loves to be handfed.
Banana Pudding gets his name because he eats smooshed bananas ONLY.

  1. Cookie
  2. Earl
  3. Banana Pudding

Alligators


Our Gators are all former mascots.
Please Note: Wren will not approach humans, only feed using the feeding pole.
Aspen is a drama-queen, make sure you feed him last. Mika is missing an eye, NEVER approach her blindside.

  1. Wren
  2. Aspen
  3. Mika
